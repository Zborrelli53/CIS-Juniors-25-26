Basic Text Formatting

Use the Strong Tag or the B Tag to make something bold

The Difference Between Strong and B

The Strong Tag is an HTML Semantic Tag. There is a meaning behind the strong tag. What that means is: Tells the browser there is something important about the text inside that tag.

The B tag means nothing. It simples bold text.

The em Tag and the I tag

The em tag and the i tag, make the text inside of them italic. Again, the difference is, the em tag is a semantic. The i tag, just turns the text italic.

The u Tag

The u Tag simply underlines your text.
