What is Bugsnax?

Bugsnax is a single player story adventure game where you play as an un-named journalist who travels to the far off island called Snaktooth Island.
You were invited by the famous adventurer Lizbert Megafig to interview the other Grumpuses that followed her on her journey to Snaktooth and discover the various creatures
that inhabit the island. These creatures are called, Bugsnax

You wake up on Snaktooth Island after a giant "Snak" destroys your hot air balloon-Ship, where you meet one of the Residents of Snaxburg,
Filbo Fiddlepie. You must then use a device called a "Snak Trap" to catch the local "Snax" and feed them to Filbo. He will then escort you to the run-down village of Snaxburg.
After interviewing "Filbo", he will give you the task of returning the rest of the Grumpuses back to "Snaxburg" and interview them.
